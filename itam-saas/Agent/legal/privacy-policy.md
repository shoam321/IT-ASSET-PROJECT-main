## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
- **Database Security:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12. Row-Level Security (RLS) enforces multi-tenant data isolation.
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures users can only access data they are authorized to view.
- **Infrastructure Security:** Our hosting providers (Railway, PostgreSQL) implement physical and network security controls.
- **Regular Backups:** Automated daily backups ensure data recovery in case of system failure.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
